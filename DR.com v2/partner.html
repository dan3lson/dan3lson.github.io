I. Web Design

Whether it's a personal, business, or an organization site, saying bringing the world to your doorstep has never been easier.

If you've never had a site before, I'll build you a new site that, among other factors,

  • looks professional and clean
  • displays well on all devices, and
  • has the essential search-engine-optimizations so that your target audience can find you

For existing site owners that want a fresh new look, I'll enhance your site by

  • making it look more appealing/modern
  • improving the user experience
  • adding new, relevant content

In both of the cases above, we'll first create user experience artifacts (e.g. detailed wireframes, low/high fidelity prototypes, and/or story boards) that show you what the site could look like before writing one line of code. After the site has been completed, we'll create a plan on how to maintain your site's freshness.
