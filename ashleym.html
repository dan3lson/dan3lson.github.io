Dove Cameron

This is a site about Dove Cameron and it´s for everybody.

Dove Cameron is a singer and actress. She was in a band called "The Girl and the Dreamcatcher", which only lasted 2 years because she and her bandmate (Ryan Mccartan) decided to break up the band and their relationship for personal reasons. She is mostly a Disney Channel star, she played both Liv AND Maddie in the show "Liv and Maddie". Dove was also Mal in the famous "Descendants" and (soon to come out), "Descendants 2.

Singing

Dove Cameron USED to be in a band called "The Girl and the Dreamcatcher". They had 1 EP called negatives which had 6 songs "Make You Stay", "Glowing in the Dark", "Gladiator", "My Way", "Cry Wolf", and "Monster". Her bandmate was also her boyfriend, Ryan McCartan. The band started in 2014 and ended in the end of 2016. Dove Cameron decided to continue singing and is in the process of making her OWN songs and album.

Acting

Dove is also an actress. She has played many roles in shows and in movies. She has been the main role in Liv and Maddie, Cloud 9, Barely Lethal, Descendants, descendants 2, R.L. Stine's Monsterville: Cabinet of Souls, Hairspray Live!, and Descendants: Wicked World. Dove played both Liv AND Maddie in the show Liv and Maddie, which had 4 seasons and lasted from 2013–2017. Dove played Amber in the 2016 Hairspray Live! Dove Cameron was in Descendants and WILL be in Descendants 2, which comes out on June 21st 2017.
