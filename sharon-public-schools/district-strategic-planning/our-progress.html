Tracking Our Progress

We monitor and publicly report our progress, as part of the district planning process, to hold ourselves accountable to the Sharon Public Schools community.

What we track is grounded in analysis of a wide array of district data, which sets specific, measurable outcomes and identifies the objectives and initiatives to accomplish them.
