Relationships/Culture Objectives

We start with our expected outcomes and then determine what actions to complete to achieve our ambitious goals. Along the way, we monitor and publicly report our progress.

Initiatives for SY 2018-2019

Diverse Staff

Expected Outcomes
Improve student, staff, family perceptions on engagement, instruction and community support

Actions
Recruit and retain diverse staff

Status

Inclusive Learning Environments

Initiatives for SY 2019-2022

Adult Connections

Actions
Provide professional development and training to build capacity in SPS staff and community stakeholders in cultural competency and core values

Inclusivity

Actions
Evaluate existing curriculum and research new curriculum and resources in all content areas to ensure cultural diversity and non-stereotypical representation of all student groups

Student Diversity

Actions
Solicit student and family feedback regarding their connectedness to and experience with Sharon Public Schools and develop students’ and families’ ability to self-advocate

Ongoing Opportunities

Actions
Partner with community to enhance understanding of cultural competency
