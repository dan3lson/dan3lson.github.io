Parents and Students

Dear parents and students, my name is Andreina Idalia Rosa, J.D. and I am a strong advocate for academic, personal and professional growth and development. I love empowering young people by not just simply advocating for their best interests, but by giving them the tools to learn how to advocate for themselves.

Students

My experience as a student from elementary school through law school inspired me to wake up every morning with confidence, purpose, and the power to believe that despite my adversities, I can still become anything I aspire to be in life. You can too. Let’s stop saying no to ourselves before even applying for that competitive school, internship, or scholarship, deterring us from at least applying for new opportunities. Or, even if we do apply, just because an opportunity doesn’t work out as planned, does not mean we give up on ourselves. When someone says no and closes a door on us, that is when we need ourselves the most to say yes. You are worthy of having big dreams for yourself. You are worthy of the process it takes to accomplish those dreams. You are worthy of living the life you want to design for yourself. But first, you must put yourself out there and put yourself in uncomfortable situations to welcome challenges and accept growth. By doing so, you will learn to understand just how powerful you really are and the impact you can have on others and on yourself. Success Worthy believes in you.

Parents

We all want what’s best for our children and sometimes we may not always have the resources, knowledge, or time to substantially dedicate ourselves to help our children become great students and working professionals the way we would like to. Success Worthy provides additional services outside of the classroom to meet the high demand of a school’s academic or college counselor who may be overworked and thus, may not provide the individualized support you desire for your child. Success Worthy works with our clients outside of school hours, including weekends to meet important deadlines whether it’s to help your child prepare for an interview for an internship or whether it’s reviewing and editing a personal statement for college or law school admissions. We are here to guide your children during all transitions from elementary school to college and beyond and we believe the most effective recipe for success is to create measurable goals and a plan that will reflect your child’s strengths, interests, and passions. Here at Success Worthy, we will follow-up with your child’s action plan to ensure completion of academic, personal, and career goals. We are here to help so that you can enjoy this time raising your children to be the best version of themselves.
